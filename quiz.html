Welcome to the Banking Comparison Tool, presented to you by Albany Law School. This tool enables our online users to select which financial institutions are best suited for them by allowing the users to place specific weights on banking categories. The summation of these weights are then used to help calculate which financial institutions are best for you. Let's give it a try!
